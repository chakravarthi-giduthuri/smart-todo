## Description (4000 chars max)
Smart To-Do uses Claude AI to turn your plain-English thoughts into organized, prioritized tasks — instantly.

**How it works**
Just type what you need to do in natural language: "Call dentist tomorrow afternoon" or "Submit quarterly report by Friday, high priority". Claude AI automatically sets the category, priority, date, time, and duration. No tapping through menus.

**Key Features**
• AI task creation — type naturally, AI does the rest
• Smart suggestions — AI recommends what to work on next based on your energy level and deadlines
• Daily planning — build and save your daily task plan with custom ordering
• Recurring templates — set up daily/weekly tasks that auto-generate
• Energy tracking — check in your energy level (high/medium/low) to get smarter suggestions
• Calendar view — see all tasks organized by date
• Focus timer — time-box individual tasks
• Push notifications — get reminded before tasks are due
• Share tasks — send a task to someone with a link
• Dark & light mode
• Works offline for viewing, syncs when connected

**Privacy**
Your tasks are stored securely in the cloud. We never sell your data.
